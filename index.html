By Humans, For Humans

An assignment for my AI class exploring the ways AI and humans collaborate in design. I aimed to create a book that acted as an introduction to AI, I had AI lay out the outline and introduction to each page then went in and researcehd to create the bulk of text within the book. In the labour of creating this book, I am thinking about the degree to which we are seperated from a truly manual world. Even without LLM's like Chat GPT, I still relied solely on large search engines which leverage AI to expose millions of resources in milliseconds. Updated images to come...
