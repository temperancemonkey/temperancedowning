Patron Saint of Revolving Doors

Patron Saint of Revolving Doors brings together text and photography to emulate the feeling of being stuck in a cycle, or in life. The book itself is spiral bound and coverless, and text on the inside makes the reader physically turn the book around and around in order to read it, thus the reader themselves recreates this circular process.

Synthetically Human

Synthetically Human explores color as a language around the city. Through image and selected text I aim to explore how even as color becomes more and more synthetic, the meaning remains the same and has for centuries.

Pictotype
Pictotype merges together several Wikipedia pages in order to recognize the evolution of symbol to type design and language today. The cover is created entirely from the letter 't' and the symbols created are dispersed throughout the book as chapter markers.
Wares
Wares, created in Glyphs, is inspired by hardware tools with an emphasis on their connection points. This typeface is ready for use upon request, and comes with a tangible specimen of laser cut acrylic pieces which can be screwed together to physically create the letters.
LTC Bolo
LTC Bolo, created in Rhino, joins the letters 'L', 'T', and 'C' to create the symbol seen on the bolo. The bolo mechanism was created in Rhino, with help from Myron Tobak.
Tried & True
Tried & True utilizes custom lettering to represent unapologetic self expression. Created in Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop.
Barrchive
I worked on Barrchive's brand identity customizing the typeface 'Nagel VF'. Cards and Bookmarks were inkjet printed and handed out at Offset Bookfair. Creating using Adobe Illustrator.
