Patron Saint of Revolving Doors

Patron Saint of Revolving Doors brings together text and photography to emulate the feeling of being stuck in a cycle, or in life. The book itself is spiral bound and coverless, and text on the inside makes the reader physically turn the book around and around in order to read it, thus the reader themselves recreates this circular process.
